Balsamic Bruschetta

Bruschetta is a toasted bread topped with tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and fresh basil.

Bruschetta Ingredients

  • 1loaf of French bread, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 salt and freshly ground black paper

Directions Step-By-Step

  1. Gather all ingredients and mix them
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  3. Brush bread with oil and toast until golden
  4. Spoon mixture onto bread slices
  5. Serve immediately
